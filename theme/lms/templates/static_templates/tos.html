Subject to the foregoing, you will be required to pay a fee and complete the ID verification process for Verified Certificates. In order to authenticate your identity for a Verified Certificate, you will be prompted to take a webcam photo of yourself, as well as a photo of an acceptable form of photo ID (described below). Acceptable forms of photo ID’s are:

  • Government or State-issued driver's license
  • Passport
  • National ID card
  • State or Province ID card (including cards issued by motor vehicle agencies)

In order to be accepted by FGYT eLearning, your photo ID must:

  • Contain your full name exactly (excluding hyphens, accents, and spaces);
  • Contain a relatively current photograph of yourself;
  • Be an original document; photocopied documents cannot be accepted; and
  • Be current and valid; expired documents cannot be accepted.

Unfortunately, if you do not possess a photo ID meeting the criteria described above, FGYT eLearning is unable to provide you with a Verified Certificate at this time.
